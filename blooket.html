Blooket

Blooket is an interactive online learning platform that turns quizzes and reviews into fun classroom games. It allows teachers to create or choose question sets and present them in different game modes where students can answer questions while competing or collaborating with classmates.


Top Features: Game-based quizzes (students play while learning), Different game modes (battle, racing, strategy), Pre-made question sets + create your own, Rewards, points, and competition, Homework mode + live classroom mode

Introduction

Blooket is less about taking a quiz and it's more about playing a game where knowledge is powered-up with different. It’s an excellent tool for reinforcing vocabulary, math facts, or historical dates in a way that feels like a reward rather than a chore.
