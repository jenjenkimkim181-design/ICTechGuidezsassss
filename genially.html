Genially

Genially is an online platform used to create interactive and visually engaging digital content. It allows users to design presentations, infographics, quizzes, interactive images, and learning materials that can include animations, links, and multimedia elements.


Top Features: Interactive presentations, infographics, and games, Animations and clickable elements, Gamification (escape rooms, quizzes, storytelling), Templates for education and business, Embed content on websites or share online

Introduction

Genially is an all-in-one online tool designed to create interactive experiences. It is widely used by educators to transform traditional content—like slides, infographics, and maps—into dynamic, clickable media that feels more like a website or a mini-game than a document.
