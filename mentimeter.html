Mentimeter

Mentimeter is an interactive presentation tool that allows presenters to engage their audience in real time. It enables teachers, students, and speakers to create presentations where participants can respond to questions, polls, quizzes, and word clouds using their smartphones or computers.


Top Features: Real-time polls, quizzes, and word clouds (instant results on screen), Live Q&A and audience reactions (emoji, comments), Professional templates + AI-assisted content, Easy access via code, link, or QR (no app needed), Data visualization (graphs, charts, insights)
